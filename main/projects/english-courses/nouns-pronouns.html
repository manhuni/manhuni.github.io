📘 Grammar: Nouns and Pronouns

Learn how to use nouns and pronouns correctly in English sentences.

1️⃣ What are Nouns?

A noun is a word that names a person, place, thing, or idea.

  • Examples: dog, city, happiness, book

2️⃣ What are Pronouns?

A pronoun is a word that replaces a noun to avoid repetition.

  • Examples: he, she, it, they

3️⃣ Example Sentences

🔹 Maria is my friend. She is very kind.

🔹 The dogs are barking. They are hungry.

4️⃣ Quick Quiz

Select the correct pronoun:

Tom is my brother. ___ loves football.
